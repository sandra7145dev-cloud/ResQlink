About ResQlink

ResQlink is a comprehensive disaster relief and rescue coordination platform designed to connect communities in need with volunteers and NGOs during times of crisis.


















Emergency Coordination

We bridge the gap between affected individuals, communities, and relief organizations, ensuring coordinated response during disasters and emergencies.

Volunteer Network

Our platform mobilizes dedicated volunteers who are ready to assist communities in need, providing essential services and support during critical times.

Resource Management

We efficiently manage and distribute essential resources including food, water, medical supplies, and shelter to disaster-affected areas.
