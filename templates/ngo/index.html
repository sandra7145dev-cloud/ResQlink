About ResQlink

ResQlink is a comprehensive disaster relief and rescue coordination platform designed to connect communities in need with dedicated NGOs and volunteers during times of crisis. Our mission is to streamline emergency response and ensure that aid reaches those who need it most, when they need it most.

As an NGO partner, you play a vital role in our ecosystem. You manage critical resources, coordinate relief efforts, and work directly with communities to provide essential services during disasters. This platform empowers you with:

  • Real-time visibility into community needs
  • Efficient resource allocation and tracking
  • Automated volunteer coordination
  • Centralized communication hub
  • Data-driven disaster response insights

Types of Disasters We Help Manage

ResQlink coordinates relief operations across multiple disaster categories to provide comprehensive support to affected communities and coordinate NGO resources effectively.

Flood Relief

Coordinating emergency response for flood-affected areas. We manage water rescue operations, shelter provisioning, medical assistance, and rehabilitation efforts for displaced communities.

Natural Disaster
Water Supply Shelter

Earthquake Response

Rapid deployment of rescue teams and medical personnel. Coordination of search & rescue operations, emergency medical care, temporary shelters, and psychological support for survivors.

Natural Disaster
Medical Rescue

Cyclone & Storm

Managing relief operations during cyclones and severe storms. Includes advance shelter arrangements, supply distribution, power restoration coordination, and recovery planning.

Natural Disaster
Infrastructure Food & Water

Landslide Recovery

Coordinating response to landslide-affected areas with challenging terrain. Management of evacuation, medical care, debris clearance, and infrastructure restoration projects.

Natural Disaster
Evacuation Rehabilitation

Health Emergency

Rapid deployment of medical resources during health crises and outbreaks. Coordination of vaccination drives, quarantine facilities, medical supplies, and community health awareness.

Health Crisis
Medical Care Testing
